About Teamo Consulting

Teamo Consulting is a 100% Black woman-owned talent development and workforce transformation consultancy.

We specialise in people development, coaching, training, HR advisory, internships, learnership management, and youth work-readiness programmes.

Our purpose is to develop skilled, confident, employable individuals and to help organisations build strong, agile, high-performing teams.

Meet the Founder

Ms Snenhlanhla Nene

People Developer | Talent Strategist | Workforce Transformation Consultant

Warm, dynamic, and deeply committed to unlocking human potential, Ms Snenhlanhla Nene is a passionate specialist in people development and workforce transformation. Her journey is rooted in a genuine love for seeing individuals rise into confident, capable leaders.

With expertise in talent development, mentoring, coaching, organisational culture, behavioural training, customer experience, and youth preparedness, she prepares young professionals for modern workspaces and builds strong leadership pipelines.

"When people grow, organisations evolve and leaders emerge."
