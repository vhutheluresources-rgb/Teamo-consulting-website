About Teamo Consulting

Teamo Consulting is a 100% Black woman-owned talent development and workforce transformation consultancy.

We specialise in people development, coaching, training, HR advisory, internships, learnership management, and youth work-readiness programmes.

Teamo Consulting is driven by a passion for people development and workforce transformation. Warm, dynamic, and deeply committed to unlocking human potential, we exist to support individuals in rising into confident, capable professionals while helping organisations build strong leadership pipelines.

With expertise in talent development, mentoring, coaching, organisational culture, behavioural training, customer experience, and youth preparedness, we prepare young professionals for modern workspaces and support organisations in creating agile, high-performing teams.
